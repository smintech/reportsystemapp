    About And Purpose Of This Site

    Ratel Anonymous Reporting Page

    This page is designed to allow you to report issues anonymously while protecting your identity.
    It is specially created for the Ratel Movement.
    All rights reserved to the creator.

    When reporting anonymously, make sure you provide all evidence and a clear explanation.
    This allows the handler to investigate your case without needing to contact you.
    In some situations, your identity may be required, but since this platform is made for the Ratel Movement, you can be assured your information is handled safely.

    All cases are managed by trusted Ratel leaders who ensure confidentiality and proper action.
    The best part of this site is if you are a witness to an incident, you can submit your report with your evidence, and the victim will be protected.

    To make your report effective:
    - Be specific and factual.
    - Attach any supporting documents, images, or videos.
    - Avoid including personal identifiers if you want to remain anonymous.
    - Keep your language clear and professional to help the handler understand your report quickly.

    Joining this platform allows you to:
    - Protect your identity while taking action.
    - Help the Ratel Movement address issues effectively.
    - Contribute to a safer and more transparent environment.
    - Be a responsible witness ensuring victims are protected.
